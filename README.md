# AirPoint-Backup
Encrypted AirPoint Backup for Customers is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data.
This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles data when using the Google Drive backup service.

1. Data We Access
The application requests access to your Google Drive for the sole purpose of creating, uploading, and updating your automated backup files.

2. Encryption & Security
To ensure maximum privacy, all backup data is fully encrypted before it is uploaded to your Google Drive.
No one, including the developers of this app, can read or access your data without your private encryption key.

4. Data Sharing
We do not collect, store, sell, or share any of your personal data or backup files on external servers.
All operations take place directly and securely between your device and your personal Google Drive account.

6. Google User Data Policy Compliance
Our app strictly complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy.
You can revoke the app's access to your Google Drive at any time through your Google Account security settings.
